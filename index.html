ABOUT US

Vekolino SI, Transport & Logistics company is based in Slovenia near Ljubljana. Our Logistic team is available 24/7 , all year long, and our Drivers are well trained, reliable, professionals which makes our company best possible choice for your cargo to be delivered.
History of our company started serving European transport system in 2007, and during that Period we connected France, Spain, Great Britain, and offering our services to many satisfied customers until 2011. Then our business expanded, and We started spreading our network of trucks to Germany and Sweden. Soon after we developed strong connection between Western Europe and Northern Europe. Today our company is part of Global Transportation Chain that connects whole World into one.

SERVICES

Our job is to connect West and North of Europe, we transport various cargo from distant parts of world to anywhere its needed. How we do it? With highest dedication, offering maximum effort and making sure everything is delivered safest and fastest possible.

  • Well equipped and reliable Trucks
